Developers

Please choose the authors.json file for the repository of your choice. You can find the file under tools/data/<repo>/authors.json location.

The first graph will depict developers and the number od their inserted lines sorted by that number.

The remaining graphs show number of good and buggy lines introduced by each developer over the course of the 4 years analysied in the project. Each of those graphs may be zoomed in to see the details. Next to the developer's email is the buggy ratio.
